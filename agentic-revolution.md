# The Agentic Revolution: Architecting Digital Sovereignty and Governance in 2026

## Introduction: The Shift from Tools to Agents
In the early 2020s, we used AI as a tool—a sophisticated search engine or a writing assistant. By 2026, the paradigm has shifted entirely toward **Agentic AI**. We are no longer just "prompting" machines; we are architecting autonomous entities capable of logic, commerce, and independent persistence. This shift brings with it complex regulatory challenges, technical requirements, and a fundamental need for digital identity ownership.
