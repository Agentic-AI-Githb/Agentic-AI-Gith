## ⚙️ Contribution Guidelines & Project Governance

We welcome developers, ethicists, philosophers, and data scientists from around the globe to participate in this grand architectural endeavor.

### How to Contribute
1. **Fork the Repository:** Create a local copy of our architecture.
2. **Review the Core Philosophy:** Before writing code, please read the Agentic Revolution and the Ethical Architecture guidelines. We do not accept contributions that compromise user privacy or circumvent Human-on-the-Loop (HOTL) safety measures.
3. **Draft Your Proposal:** Whether it is an update to our high-traffic scaling protocols or a new chapter in the biological discovery matrix, submit an issue detailing your intent.
4. **Peer and Agent Review:** Your code will be reviewed by human maintainers and analyzed by our internal code-quality agents to ensure security and alignment with our Italian and EU regulatory frameworks.

### Code of Conduct
We enforce a strict environment of respect, scientific rigor, and collaborative empathy. The deployment of AI systems holds immense power; therefore, the individuals building them must adhere to the highest standards of professional integrity.
